Valters Edvards Andersons - UX/UI designer

My name is Valters and currently I am a student at UCN multimedia class, but my roots in design started way back when I was creating advertising for local concerts and menus for restaurants. I am excellent team player with great experience from team-based games like basketball and I have had spent many years in countless orchestras, musical ensembles and other activities, which has greatly improved my communication skills. I have passion for UX/UI, project management and design in general, so if that’s what you are looking for why don’t we get in touch?
